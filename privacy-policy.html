Companion RafiQ ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you may provide. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Companion RafiQ does not directly collect or store any personal information. All data you enter in the app is stored locally on your device.

If you enable HealthKit integration, Companion RafiQ may access and write nutrition or fasting data to Apple's HealthKit. Companion RafiQ does not store or share this data externally; it remains solely within your Health app.

Third-party Services

Companion RafiQ does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Security

Your privacy and security are our priorities. Data entered is stored securely on your device.
